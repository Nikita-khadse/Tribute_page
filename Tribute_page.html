Within the mind are all the resources required for successful living. Ideas are present in the consciousness, which when released and given scope to grow and take shape, can lead to successful events.

Biography

APJ Abdul Kalam is one of the most celebrated men of India. He is beloved by everyone, as a president, as a scientist, as an inspiration, Abdul Kalam was a man of many talents. APJ Abdul Kalam’s contribution to Indian space research as an aerospace scientist is remarkable. He inspires lots of children of India to work hard and fulfill their dream. He became the 11th president of India in the year 2002 and served the country as the president for the next five years, that is to say, till 2007. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam is known as the missile man of India because of his contribution to the project of Missiles. The name of the two main projects of the missile which is launched by India, which are named “Agni” and “Prithvi”. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was one of the leading scientists of the ISRO, that is to say, the Indian Space Research Organisation, and also of DRDO, which stands for Defence Research and Development Organisation. In the “Pokhran II” nuclear test, which was tested in the year 1998, and is known as the second original nuclear test by India, he played a crucial role in it as well.
